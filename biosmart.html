01 / Components

Four components. One system.

BioSmart isn't a product — it's the protocol our teams run on every visit. Each component does work the others can't. Combined, they replace most traditional cleaning chemistry without trading off results.

Component 01

Hypochlorous acid.

HOCl · Disinfection

The same compound your white blood cells produce to fight infection — generated on-site from salt, water, and electricity. Hospital-grade disinfection at point-of-use, no harsh residue, safe on food-contact surfaces. Used for kitchen counters, bathroom sanitization, high-touch surfaces, and medical clinic surfaces. Health Canada recognizes hypochlorous acid as a sanitizer at the concentrations we deploy.

Component 02

Enzyme cleaners.

Bio-active · Organic waste

Live enzymes that digest organic material at the molecular level — pet stains, urine, grease, food residue. They keep working after wipe-down, continuing to break down what surface cleaning missed. 100% biodegradable, septic-safe, no harsh fumes. Particularly effective for pet accidents, drain maintenance, and odor-source elimination. Replaces ammonia-based and quat-based cleaners in most residential applications.

Component 03

Colour-coded microfibre.

4-zone system

Microfibre captures particles smaller than one micron using mechanical action rather than chemical solvents. For many surfaces — glass, mirrors, finished wood, electronics — water plus the correct microfibre is the complete protocol. Colour codes prevent cross-contamination between zones: red for bathrooms, blue for kitchens, green for general surfaces, yellow for high-touch and shared. Industry standard in healthcare and food safety.

Component 04

HEPA filtration.

Sealed vacuum systems

HEPA-rated filters capture 99.97% of airborne particles down to 0.3 microns — the size where most dust mites, pet dander, mold spores, and pollen sit. Our backpack vacuums are fully sealed, meaning the air pulled in doesn't get pushed back out into the room. Critical for allergens, asthma triggers, and fine construction dust. Operates on every visit, not as an upcharge.

03 / Who benefits

Built for spaces with sensitive populations.

BioSmart was designed for the homes and businesses where traditional chemistry was already a problem — and it became the default for everyone else. We don't charge extra. There's no "BioSmart tier." It's how every clean runs.

Households with young children.

Kids touch every surface, then their faces. Lower chemical residue means less exposure during play, meals, and crawling. No "ventilate the room before letting them back in."

Homes with pets.

Dogs and cats walk on floors, then lick their paws. Birds are especially sensitive to airborne chemicals. Enzyme cleaners remove the source of pet accidents — they don't just mask the smell.

Asthma, allergies, sensitivities.

Fragrance is a documented asthma trigger. Quats and bleach can aggravate respiratory conditions. BioSmart runs fragrance-free and replaces those chemistries directly. HEPA filtration handles airborne triggers.

Medical & dental clinics.

Patient-facing surfaces, sterilization-adjacent areas, and shared waiting rooms need hospital-grade disinfection without harsh residue. Hypochlorous acid is the standard in infection-control protocols.

Daycares & preschools.

High-touch surfaces, shared toys, food-prep areas. Provincial daycare standards require safe sanitizers — BioSmart meets the documentation requirements most operators are asked to maintain.

Food prep & restaurants.

Counters, prep stations, dish areas. Food-contact safe is the requirement — hypochlorous acid clears that bar at the concentrations we deploy, with no rinse required between disinfection and food contact.
