Why this matters

Across Alberta, move-out standards are the most-cited source of friction between landlords and tenants. A documented clean — with photo evidence — moves the conversation from "I think it's clean enough" to "here's the report."

03 / Two audiences

For tenants. For owners.

The clean is the same. The conversation it ends is different. Whichever side of the lease you're on, the documented report is the part that matters most.

For tenants & renters

Get the unit to a defensible standard.

Alberta's Residential Tenancies Act says you owe "ordinary cleanliness" when you vacate. The hard part isn't the cleaning — it's proving you did it. Our photo report is the evidence. Forward it to your landlord with your forwarding address.

  • extended 100-point checklistscope brings every surface to baseline
  • 40+ timestamped photos, geotagged to the unit
  • Written report sent to your email + downloadable PDF
  • Common in tenancy disputes — judges accept dated photo evidence
  • Doesn't cover physical damage (that's between you and your landlord)
