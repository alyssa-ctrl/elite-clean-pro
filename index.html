BioSmart Technology is Elite Clean Pro's proprietary cleaning system, built around four science-backed components. Enzyme-based cleaners biologically break down soils instead of dissolving them with harsh chemicals. Electrolyzed water generates hospital-grade disinfection from salt and water — safer than bleach, and surface-safe for natural stone, hardwood, and screens. Nano-fibre microfibre traps particles 100x smaller than a human hair. Electrostatic sprayers deliver charged-particle disinfectant coverage on commercial spaces. The kit replaces fifteen-plus traditional cleaning chemicals with eight focused, low-toxicity tools — better for Calgary homes with kids, pets, allergies, and asthma, and dramatically more effective on the hard-water deposits common to Calgary's 200–250 mg/L water.

The Elite Score is Elite Clean Pro's proprietary post-clean quality metric, calculated on a 100-point scale from five components: 47-point checklist completion (60% weight), surface inspection pass rate (15%), time-on-task efficiency against spec (10%), supply utilization (5%), and client confirmation (10%). Our system average is 97.3% across audited cleans. Every visit is scored, logged, and reviewed before the team leaves your space. If your space scores under 95%, we return the next day at no charge — no questions, no negotiation. The Elite Score is reported to clients after each clean, so you see exactly what was completed and where the team flagged anything for follow-up.
