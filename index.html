Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Do you charge extra for pets or large homes? +
Homes with pets may have an additional $15–25 fee for extra hair and dander removal. Large homes over 3,000 sqft are quoted individually — contact us for a custom quote.
Is there a discount for recurring bookings? +
Yes. Weekly cleans get 15% off, bi-weekly cleans get 10% off, and monthly cleans get 5% off. Discounts are applied automatically at booking.
What if I'm not happy with my clean? +
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the areas in question at no charge.
Do you bring your own supplies and equipment? +
Yes, we bring everything. All our products are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and safe for children and pets. If you have specific product preferences, just let us know.
How do commercial cleaning contracts work? +
Commercial contracts are month-to-month with no lock-in. We'll assess your space, provide a custom quote, and set up a cleaning schedule that works around your business hours.
Are you insured and what areas do you cover? +
Yes, Elite Clean Pro is fully insured and bonded in Alberta. We cover all of Calgary including NW, NE, SW, SE, Beltline, Kensington, Bridgeland, and surrounding areas like Airdrie, Cochrane, and Chestermere.
