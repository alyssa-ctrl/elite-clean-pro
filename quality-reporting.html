01 / Anatomy

What's in every report.

Six things, every time. No variation. No skipped sections. The report is the same shape whether you're in a 1-bedroom condo or a 4-floor commercial building — only the rooms change.

Element 01

The 100-point checklist.

Forty-seven discrete checkpoints per room, scored as the team works. Not a sampling, not an audit, not a generated summary. The full list, every visit, on every room. Read the full system in The Standard.
Element 02

Photo evidence.

Every kitchen, every bathroom, every floor — photographed at completion. Timestamped at capture. Geotagged to your address. Stored in your client portal indefinitely. You see what we saw when we left.
Element 03

The Elite Score.

A composite out of 100, calculated from five components: checklist completion (60%), surface inspection (15%), time-on-task efficiency (10%), supply utilization (5%), client confirmation (10%). System average across audited cleans: 97.3%.
Element 04

Team lead note.

A named person — not a shared inbox, not a bot — leaves a short note on what they observed. Anything flagged for next visit. Anything noticed in your space that you should know about. The voice that explains the number.
Element 05

Action items.

Things flagged for your attention — a leaky faucet, a worn baseboard, a cracked tile. Not part of our work, but you should know. Photographed when visible. We don't fix them. We don't pretend we didn't see them.
Element 06

Delivery details.

Sent via email within an hour of team departure. Available in your client portal indefinitely. Downloadable as PDF. If any room scores under 95, the report includes the re-clean booking link — confirmed by The Promise.
02 / Delivery

From arrival to inbox.

A typical visit. Times vary by property size, but the sequence — and the report at the end — does not.

0:00Arrival

Team arrives.

Automatic notification sent at 30 minutes out. Geofenced arrival check-in starts the timer. Team enters with the access notes you provided at booking.

0:05 – 2:00Work

Clean and score, in parallel.

Team works the 100-point checklist room by room, scoring as they go. Photo evidence captured at the completion of each room — not the end of the visit.

2:00 – 2:10Review

Team lead reviews against the standard.

Walkthrough against the checklist. Any rooms flagged below 95 trigger re-work on the spot. Lead writes the note while observations are fresh.

~2:45Inbox

Report in your inbox.

Generated automatically at team departure. Email and client portal. PDF download. You see the full report before you walk back through the door.

Times approximate · Sample visit on a 4-bedroom residence
