01 / Pillars

Why facility managers switch.

Most Calgary commercial cleaners compete on price and credentials. We compete on the five things experienced facility managers actually evaluate — the ones that determine whether the partnership lasts year over year.

Pillar 01

Documented QC.

Every visit scored against a 100-point checklist. Photo evidence captured per location. Written report archived. Not "we'll check on it." A repeatable system that runs the same regardless of who's on shift.

Pillar 02

Named contact.

One account manager owns your relationship from day one. Direct email, direct phone. No shared inbox, no ticket queue, no escalation chain. You know who to write — and they know you.

Pillar 03

Crew consistency.

Same A+ team on every visit. Trained on your specific space, your access protocols, your priorities. If a member is unavailable, you receive notice and the substitute's profile in advance. No surprise cleaners.

Pillar 04

Real communication.

24-hour response SLA, seven days. Monthly review with your named contact. Scheduled walkthroughs. The partnership feels managed, not improvised — even when something unexpected happens.

Pillar 05

Compliance ready.

. $25K bonding. WCB Alberta on every team member. Commercial auto liability. Certificate of Insurance issued within one business day, with your insurer's exact wording.
