Common Questions

Pricing FAQ.

Calgary cleaning prices range from $120 to $300 per visit at most providers. Elite Clean Pro charges flat per-visit rates from $139 (1–2 bedroom) to $219+ (5+ bedroom), with recurring discounts down to $118 per visit on a weekly schedule.
Hourly billing rewards slow cleaning. Flat-rate billing rewards efficient cleaning to a defined standard. The 47-Point System defines what "done" looks like for every room. The price covers the whole job, not the time it takes.
Every visit includes the BioSmart Technology kit, the full 47-Point System checklist, an Elite Score quality report, and the same A+ team on recurring visits. No supply fee, no fuel charge.
No long-term contract. Recurring rates apply as long as you stay on the schedule. Pause or cancel anytime with one visit's notice.
Homes with pets may have an additional $15–25 fee for extra hair and dander removal. Homes over 3,000 sqft are quoted individually.
All of Calgary — NW, NE, SW, SE, Beltline, Kensington, Bridgeland, Mission — plus Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and Okotoks. Same-week availability across the city.
