Pricing Questions

Pricing FAQ.

House cleaning in Calgary in 2026 costs between $120 and $300 per visit at most providers. Elite Clean Pro charges flat rates from $139 per visit for a 1–2 bedroom home (one-time) or $118 per visit on a weekly recurring plan. A 3–4 bedroom home starts at $179 one-time or $152 recurring weekly. These are all-in prices — no supply fee, no fuel surcharge, no hourly minimum.
Commercial office cleaning in Calgary typically costs $800–$2,500 per month depending on size and frequency. Elite Clean Pro charges $800/month for offices up to 2,000 sqft (3 visits per week) and $1,800/month for mid-size offices of 2,000–5,000 sqft (5 visits per week). Larger facilities receive a custom written quote within 48 hours.
Hourly billing rewards slow cleaning. Flat-rate billing rewards efficient cleaning to a defined standard. The 100-point system defines exactly what "done" looks like for every room. The flat rate covers the whole job — not the time it takes. You know your price before the team arrives, not after.
Every visit at any price includes: the full BioSmart Technology cleaning kit (enzyme-based cleaners, electrolyzed water disinfection, nano-fibre microfibre), the 100-point system room-by-room checklist, an Elite Score post-clean quality report, and the same A+ team on recurring visits. No supply fee, no fuel charge, no hidden extras.
No long-term contract. Recurring rates apply as long as you stay on schedule. You can pause, skip, or cancel anytime with one visit's notice. The discounted recurring rate is locked in for the duration of your schedule.
Homes with pets may have an additional $15–25 fee for extra hair and dander removal. This is disclosed upfront in your written quote — never added as a surprise after the visit. Homes over 3,000 sqft are quoted individually.
Elite Clean Pro publishes flat-rate pricing online without requiring an in-home consultation — Merry Maids and Molly Maid Calgary both require one. Elite Clean Pro bills flat per visit; both competitors use hourly billing with a minimum charge. Elite Clean Pro assigns the same team every visit; both competitors rotate teams. Elite Clean Pro provides a written Elite Score quality report after every clean; competitors offer re-clean guarantees instead.
