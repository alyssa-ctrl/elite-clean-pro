03 / Damage Process

If we break something.

Things go wrong. A wine glass slips, a fragile frame falls, a delicate finish marks. We don't pretend it doesn't happen. We publish exactly what we do when it does — so you know what to expect before signing on.

01

Tell us within 48 hours.

Email your named account contact, or reply to the quality report. Send photos of the damage. The 48-hour window keeps the timeline tight enough to investigate fairly — for both sides.

02

We acknowledge within 24 hours.

A written reply from your named contact — no shared inbox, no automated response. We confirm we've received the report and outline next steps in plain English.

03

We review with the team.

Lead and team are interviewed. Photos from the quality report are reviewed against your photos. We're looking for the truth — not for ways to dispute the claim.

04

We respond with one of three outcomes.

Outcome A: We repair or replace at our cost. Fast, no paperwork. Outcome B: We file with our insurance and manage the claim — you never call adjusters. Outcome C: We explain in writing why we believe the damage was not caused by our team, with evidence. You can dispute. We can mediate.

05

Resolution within 14 days.

For Outcome A and C: resolved in 14 days from report. For Outcome B (insurance claims), 30 days is typical — but we manage the adjuster relationship, not you.

05 / FAQ

Frequently asked, directly answered.

The questions Calgary customers most often ask before signing. If yours isn't here, write to your named contact — we'll answer and add it.

Is Elite Clean Pro background-checked?

Yes. Commercial General Liability, against theft, WCB Alberta coverage on every team member, and commercial auto liability. Certificate of Insurance available on request for commercial accounts within one business day.

What if your team damages something in my home?

Report within 48 hours of the clean. Your named account contact acknowledges within 24 hours. We respond with one of three documented outcomes — repair or replace at our cost, file with our insurance and manage the claim ourselves, or explain in writing why we believe the damage wasn't caused by our team. How do I cancel my cleaning service?

Recurring service: any time, no fee, no notice required. One-time bookings: by 8pm the day before for no charge; same-day cancellations are 50%. No subscription lock, no membership fee. Full cancellation terms.

What is the satisfaction guarantee?

Every clean is scored room-by-room against the 100-point checklist system. If any room scores under 95, we return the next day at no charge to re-clean. No questions, no negotiation, no escalation required. The booking link arrives with your quality report.

How quickly do you respond to complaints?

Within 24 hours, seven days a week. Your named account contact — a real person, not a shared inbox or bot — sends the first reply. Most replies arrive in under 4 hours during business days.

Do I get the same cleaning team every visit?

Yes, for recurring clients. The same A+ team is assigned to your space across visits — same lead, same protocol, same priorities. If a team member is unavailable, you receive written notice and the substitute's profile before they arrive. No surprise cleaners.
